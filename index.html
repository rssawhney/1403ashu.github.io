About Us

This is what we are.

We-Convert provides a customer oriented service by automating the process of waste disposal. Also the user is rewarded with credits (rewards) when he chooses to use our E-bins for garbage disposal. The three R’s to our service are:
Register-by providing 10-digit mobile number
Recycle-by discarding inorganic scrap

Vision

With its unmatched technology and approach we convert aims to cover every segment of effective and efficient waste disposal.
To promote Swachh Bharat Abhiyan is the primary aim of our company and to work rigorously to show remarkable results.
